Official Description

High-Caliber Ambition

DARE Lab is a global, decentralized non-profit community of ambitious young and high-caliber aviation researchers dedicated to producing top-quality scientific outputs across the aviation ecosystem—spanning air transport, ATM, airport operations, safety, and sustainability.

We empower early-career talent alongside established experts, sharing datasets, simulation environments, and domain expertise globally.

Network Model

Decentralized.

Designed for flexibility and scale. Topic-driven working groups enable cross-pollination between teams and faster translation of ideas into publications.

Priority

Quality-First.

Rigorous standards in research design, methodological transparency, and reproducibility aiming for high-impact scholarly assets.

Roadmap

Building an international footprint.

Focusing on regular webinars, focused workshops, and producing edited volumes that set new directions for the aviation research community.

Ethics & Legal Framework
Legal Disclaimer

Institutional Independence.

DARE Lab is a decentralized research collective. Institutional affiliations of members are for identification purposes only. The Lab does not legally represent the universities or organizations where its members are employed. All research findings, opinions, and outputs are solely the responsibility of the individual authors and do not necessarily reflect the official policies or positions of their respective institutions.

Ethical Code

COPE Guidelines.

We adhere to the Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE) guidelines. Our members commit to intellectual honesty, data integrity, and transparent conflict-of-interest disclosures in all scholarly activities.

Limited Liability

The DARE Lab provides a collaborative platform for academic exchange. Under no circumstances shall the Lab or its founders be held liable for any direct, indirect, or consequential damages arising from the use or interpretation of the research outputs or datasets provided on this platform.
